The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation's trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Bangladesh 2.0 continued to underscore the resilience and determination of the nation's youth in addressing critical social and political issues. Thousands of students took to the streets, voicing demands for affordable education, improved road safety, and greater accountability from authorities. These movements, driven by frustrations over economic inequality and systemic inefficiencies, highlighted the power of collective action in sparking national dialogue. Despite challenges such as police crackdowns and administrative resistance, the students remained steadfast, leveraging social media and grassroots organization to amplify their voices. Their protests not only showcased their commitment to justice and equity but also underscored the potential of the younger generation to shape a more inclusive and progressive future for Bangladesh".

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
